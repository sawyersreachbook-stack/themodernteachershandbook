The Educator Behind the Handbook

Nick Duda (M.S. Ed) is a battle-tested educator who has achieved six consecutive "Excellent" overall ratings—the highest tier possible—over a 22-year career.

His experience spans the full spectrum of American education. Nick has taught in highly ranked affluent districts, high-poverty urban districts influenced by ESL and gang dynamics, and rural Illinois districts. Having taught Math, Language Arts, Science, Social Studies, and 5th Grade, he understands the unique pressure points of every subject and every student demographic.

Beyond his classroom success, Nick successfully revamped a district-wide mentorship program. As a trained coach, he now focuses on helping teachers move away from burnout and toward a culture of fulfillment.
