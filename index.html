The Educator Behind the Handbook

Nick Duda (M.S. Ed) is a veteran educator and trained coach on a mission to help teachers reclaim the joy in their profession.

With 22 years of classroom experience, Nick has achieved six consecutive "Excellent" overall ratings—the highest tier of professional evaluation. Beyond his own classroom success, Nick has served as a dedicated New Teacher Mentor and successfully revamped a district-wide mentorship program to better support and retain incoming staff.

As a trained teacher coach, he combines battle-tested strategies with a deep focus on atmosphere and emotional well-being, ensuring that educators don't just survive their careers, but thrive in them.
